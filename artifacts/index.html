Artifacts

Placer, out-of-order CPU, FPGA accelerator, custom multiplier, and bare-metal STM32 firmware.

  • Artifact 1 — Advanced Standard-Cell Placer
    Multi-threaded VLSI floorplanner in Rust: B*-tree representation, simulated annealing, and timing- and congestion-aware cost terms.
  • Artifact 2 — Tomasulo Out-of-Order RISC-V CPU
    Synthesizable RV32I CPU with register renaming, reservation stations, and a reorder buffer — taken from RTL through OpenLane to a Sky130 GDSII.
  • Artifact 3 — Polynomial Evaluation Accelerator
    SystemVerilog dataflow accelerator with five cooperating FSMs, dual-port coefficient memory, and 100% functional coverage against a C reference.
  • Artifact 4 — 6-Bit Dadda-Optimized Multiplier
    ENEE640 static-CMOS multiplier: Dadda reduction, Verilog verification, and delay/power analysis; full write-up in Exam 1 report (PDF).
  • Artifact 5 — STM32 Bare-Metal Drivers (MoT)
    ENEE440 STM32G491 “Micro on Tether”: register-level assembly drivers, serial test harness, and paired user + maintainer manuals on GitHub.
